Marceline's Bite Cocktail Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Strawberry rum
  • .5 oz Whipped cream vodka
  • .5 oz UV cake vodka
  • Grenadine
  • Sprite

Directions

Add all alcohol to a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously with ice. Strain into a old fashion glass. Fill with Sprite. Slowly sink grenadine to the bottom and garnish with a strawberry.
