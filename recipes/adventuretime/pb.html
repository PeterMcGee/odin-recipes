Princess Bubbletini Cocktail Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Bubble gum vodka
  • .5 oz Amaretto
  • 1 splash Grenadine (to color)
  • Sprite

Directions

In a cocktail shaker add all alcohol and a splash of grenadine. Shake vigorously with ice. Strain into a martini glass. Fill with Sprite. Garnish with a gumball.
